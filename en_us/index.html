team introduction

XTC development team, full name XiaotongCode development team (hereinafter referred to as XTC development team), was created by Minecraft anchor (not great) and developer Xiaotong MC. It was established in Chongqing in December 2022, and a total of 3 developers participated in the development in the initial stage. In March 2023, the Minecraft NetEase version component was released for the first time. Xiaotong MC has posted content about the XTC development team on station B and actively strives for better development.
